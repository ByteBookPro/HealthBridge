## Description (4000 chars)

HealthBridge Vault is the only health platform built for the **multi-watch era**. Whether you switch from Apple Watch to a Galaxy, alternate a Garmin for trail runs and a Fitbit for sleep, or just want to keep a lifetime of metrics safe from any single vendor, HealthBridge keeps every step, heartbeat, and sleep cycle in one encrypted, AI-aware home.

**Why HealthBridge Vault?**

• **Cross-brand sync** — Apple Watch, Samsung Galaxy Watch, Garmin, Fitbit, Polar, Whoop, Oura. One dashboard, one history.
• **Watch migration in minutes** — Switching watches? Our Migration Wizard moves every metric across brands without losing a day.
• **AI Health Insights (PRO)** — GPT-powered analysis spots patterns, anomalies, and trends you'd never notice on your own.
• **Encrypted vault** — Local biometric lock + AES-encrypted exports. Your health data never trains anyone's model.
• **Smart notification bridge** — Pick exactly which apps mirror to your wrist. Quiet hours and per-app rules.
• **Goal tracking with weekly reports (PRO)** — Set steps, sleep, heart, exercise goals and get a personalized weekly score.
• **One-tap export** — JSON, CSV, or GPX. Take your data anywhere. No lock-in, ever.

**Free vs PRO**

Free: Cross-brand sync, dashboard, notification bridge, watch migration, JSON/CSV export.
PRO ($4.99/mo, 30-day free trial): AI Insights, Goals + Weekly Report, GPX export, advanced conflict resolution, unlimited history.

**Privacy first**

We don't sell your data. We don't share it with advertisers. Everything is end-to-end TLS, locally biometric-gated, and yours to export or delete at any time.

Made by humans who got tired of starting from zero every time they switched watches.
